## HR Attrition Analysis
**Tool:** Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)

### Dataset: IBM HR Analytics (1,470 employees)

### Key Findings:
- Total employees: 1,470
- Employees left: 237
- Overall attrition rate: 16.12%
- Highest attrition department: Sales (20.6%)
- Highest attrition age group: 18-25 (35%)
- Avg salary of employees who left is LOWER than those who stayed
- Low job satisfaction (Level 1) = highest attrition at 23%
